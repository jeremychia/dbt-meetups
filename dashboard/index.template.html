What the community is talking about

Every talk is tagged with topics, then rolled up into one of 8 categories by its primary topic. Click a slice or legend row to see which topics it contains and sample talks, so you can validate the classification yourself.

How this is computedEach talk's topic list comes from AI-assisted enrichment of the meetup description; the first topic listed is treated as the "primary" topic and mapped to one of 8 categories via a fixed lookup table. A talk is counted once, under its primary category only.
